Mexican Sopes

Description

Sopes (soh-pez) are a Mexican antojito made of masa, water, oil, and salt.

To make sopes, the first step is to prepare the masa into the shape of plate.
After forming the plate base, you are free to add any toppings you like!
In my household we typically add refried beans as first layer followed by ground beef.

Ingredients

  • Masa
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

How are Sopes prepared?

  1. Make the dough
  2. Divide the dough into uniform balls
  3. Flatten each ball of dough with a tortilla press
  4. Fry each flattened piece of dough in a skillet with oil
  5. Mold fried Sopes using your fingers after excess oil has been removed
  6. Add your choice of toppings and enjoy
